About Me

Retail operations with an entrepreneur's mindset.

I am currently working hands-on in my family's apparel business, where I have learned how retail actually works: customer behavior, margins, inventory movement, and the details that determine whether something sells or sits.

Alongside that, I am building Banom, a brand focused on comfortable everyday clothing with a distinct personality. From product selection to branding and customer experience, I stay involved in every step.

What drives me is learning by doing. I am interested in how brands are built from scratch, how customers choose one product over another, and how small decisions compound into durable growth.

Operating Principles

What I optimize for while building and learning.

01

Clarity over noise

The stronger the positioning, the less a brand needs to explain itself. I value simple ideas expressed well.

02

Customer behavior is the real brief

What customers notice, trust, hesitate on, or come back for matters more than what sounds good in a pitch.

03

Small decisions compound

Packaging, tone, product selection, and experience each look minor alone, but together they shape a brand's memory.
