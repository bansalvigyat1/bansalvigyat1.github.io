About Me

Retail operations with an entrepreneur's mindset.

I am currently working hands-on in my family's apparel business, where I have learned how retail really works: customers, margins, inventory, and everything in between.

Alongside that, I am building Banom, a brand focused on comfortable, everyday clothing with a distinct personality. From product selection to branding and customer experience, I am involved in every step.

What drives me is simple: learning by doing. Not just studying business, but actually running one.

I am especially interested in understanding how brands are built from scratch, what makes customers choose one product over another, and how small decisions compound into real growth.

Always open to conversations, collaborations, and opportunities where I can learn and contribute.
